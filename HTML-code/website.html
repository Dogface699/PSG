This Project Celebrates the Legacy, The Ultras, The Nights, and the Never-Fading Brilliance of Paris Saint-Germain

The ultras of Paris Saint-Germain, led by the Collectif Ultras Paris (CUP), are the beating heart of the Parc des Princes. On matchday, they transform the stadium with thunderous chants, striking tifos, and electric energy that surges through the crowd. Whether it’s a domestic clash or a European showdown, their fierce loyalty and fiery presence drive the team forward. When PSG scores, the eruption isn’t just celebration—it’s unity, devotion, and pure Parisian pride.

Paris Saint-Germain is not just a football club—it’s a force of nature, one of the greatest teams to ever grace the pitch. Legends of the game have stepped onto the fearsome stage of the Parc des Princes, from the dazzling dribbles of Ronaldinho and Neymar to the thunderous finishes of Cavani, the genius of Messi, and the lightning-fast teenage brilliance of Kylian Mbappé. Yet for years, despite their star-studded squads and breathtaking football, the prestigious UEFA Champions League trophy remained elusive. The Parc des Princes has witnessed unforgettable nights under the floodlights, where the roar of the crowd shakes the very foundations of the stadium. The ultras—some of the most passionate and extreme in world football—ignite the atmosphere with flares, chants, and unwavering devotion. As the commentator once cried, “Ici c’est Paris!”—this is Paris, and this is where magic happens. Another echoed, “Paris est magique!”, capturing the essence of a club that blends elegance with fire. Then came the night that changed everything: January 22, 2025, when PSG hosted Manchester City in the Champions League. Trailing 0–2 early in the second half, the team unleashed a comeback for the ages. Dembele struck first, followed by Barcola’s equalizer, Neves’ towering header, and finally Gonçalo Ramos sealing the 4–2 victory in stoppage time2. The commentator’s voice rang out: “Joao Neves rises like a phoenix—PSG lead! The Parc explodes!” That night, the Champions League dream was no longer a dream—it was reality. Since that iconic match, PSG has been unstoppable. Four trophies, an unbeaten league run, and a squad that blends youth, experience, and brilliance. The manager commands with tactical mastery, the players perform with heart and flair, and the fans—oh, the fans—are the heartbeat of it all. The 2025 PSG team will be remembered not just for winning, but for the drama, the passion, the roars, and the resilience that brought glory to Paris. This is more than football. This is PSG.
